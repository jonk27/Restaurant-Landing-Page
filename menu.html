Rice
  • Bibimbap | 비빔밥 | 拌饭

    A classic Korean rice bowl consisting of fresh spinach, bean sprouts, leek, marinated beef, and covered in a sweet and spicy mix of sesame oil and gochujang.

  • Spicy Chicken Stone Pot Bibimbap | 매운닭 돌솥 | 鸡肉石锅拌饭

    Served in a hot to the touch stone bowl, with spicy marinated chicken thigh on top of a bed of rice and fresh vegetables.

  • Spicy Squid Fried Rice 오징어 볶음밥 | 鱿鱼石锅拌饭

    Fresh-caught baby octopus fried with rice and seasoned with a Korean spice blend including Korean chili flakes(gochugaru), gochujang, and pepper. A perfect mix of spice and sea.

Soups
  • Gamja Tang | 감자탕 | 猪骨干土豆汤

    Gamja-tang or pork back-bone stew is a spicy Korean soup made from the spine or neck bones of a pig. It contains Napa cabbage and a potato.

  • Soon Dae Gook | 순대국 | 米肠汤

    SoonDaeGuk includes a kind of blood sausage made with intestine stuffed with pig's blood and other ingredients.

  • Daeji Gook Bop | 돼지국밥 | 猪肉汤饭

    Guk, also sometimes known as tang, is a class of soup-like dishes in Korean cuisine. It is one of the most basic components in a Korean meal, along with Bap (rice), and banchan (side dishes)

Specials
  • Wang Jokbal | 왕족발 | 王猪手

    Jokbal is a Korean dish consisting of pig's trotters cooked with soy sauce and spices. It is usually braised in a combination of soy sauce and ginger.

  • Pork Belly Platter(BoSSam) | 보쌈 | 菜包肉

    BoSsam is a Korean dish consisting of steamed pork and kimchi, which is eaten with various sorts of sauce and vegetables.

  • Soondae (Korean Blood Sausage) | 순대 | 米肠

    Soondae is a type of blood sausage in Korean cuisine. It is a popular street food in both North and South Korea, generally made by steaming cow or pig's intestines stuffed with various ingredients.

About

This seemingly nondescript restaurant has been drawing customers by word of mouth for its meaty and gelatinous boiled pig’s trotters since 2007. The business exploded, and the restaurant operated four additional locations nearby at one point, but chose to just focus on the original location in the name of quality control. Boiled pork belly and chilled buckwheat noodles in kimchi broth are also popular items on the menu. Open till late.
